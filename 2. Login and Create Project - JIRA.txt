	b. login by gmail account
	c. type your site name "myjiraproject-01"
	d. What kind of work do you do: Software development
	e. How does your team plan to use Jira: Track bugs, Manage task, Prioritize work
	f. Name your project: project-01      How familiar are you with Jira: Not familiar. Now click on "Get started" button
